About us

1. Where’s SelfWealth located?

We’re based in Surrey Hills, Melbourne — not the Sydney Surry Hills! As we’re an online-only business, we’re not set up to take customers in person. Best to talk to us on live chat or via email!

2. Why SelfWealth was started?

For decades, investment information was controlled by the few. If you managed to get access to a broker, you then paid excessive fees. We set out to make the stock market more accessible to Australians, by introducing Australia's first flat-fee share trading platform.

3. Who owns SelfWealth?

You can own SelfWealth. We’re publicly listed (ASX:SWF) and over one third of the company is owned by SelfWealth members. Otherwise, the founder and local institutional investors have a stake in the business. We’re independent from banks.

4. The people

SelfWealth is lead by Managing Director, Rob Edgley, who has experience in international finance and investment banking in Australia, the UK and Japan. He's also a Tigers fan, but we don't hold that against him.

The management team is then made up by Jarrod in marketing, Tania in compliance, Andrew in tech, Brendan in sales and Matt in the client services team. The full team is about 20 people and we’re all Melbourne locals!

5. Company values

The company was founded on delivering value and fairness to Australian investors. These values still permeate through the SelfWealth culture, over 8 years on. As Australian investors ourselves, we want to create a great product and ensure we’re helping you achieve your financial goals.
